    • HELP US

    Dibecare is a voluntary organisation with a fantastic team of volunteers to who we are most grateful. Without our volunteers we wouldn't be able to deliver our services. If you have time to spare on a regular basis we have a variety of ways in which you can help. If however, you are unable to commit on a regular basis but would like to help we are always looking for ways of fundraising. Please have a look at our services page to see the services opportunities we have at the moment.
    In order to carry out our work we not only rely on the tremendous volunteer force but the generosity of our financial and material supporters.
    You can also donate by PayPal or set up a standing order with your own bank to make a regular donation, please get in touch for details.
