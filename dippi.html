    About me

    Hi, I am Lollo! My tasks regard the activity I like most: teamwork. I love seeing my guys cooperate to reach the same goal and I'm always available to help them to face every obstacle they may encounter. Solving problems and overcoming difficulties together are priceless resources, not only here but everywhere!

    I have been helping people working together since I was young. My first experiences date back to the holidays organized by my oratory, where I was a children entertainer. In my curriculum you can see many other similar experiences I did from that time.

    Here at Dibecare I am the supervisor of all the projects that are developed by our guys during my activity. Some of them are exposed in certain parts of the campus, making it even more beautiful than how it already is.

    To me there is no better feeling than the satisfaction to see my guys sharing together the final result of their hard work.
