    About me

    Hi, I am Dibe! I am the director of Dibecare and together with the staff I put all my effort to make this world a wonderful place to have fun, learn, build new relationships and work together. I have the honor to lead a team of awesome people, whom I will never thank enough for the energy they spend daily to make this dream a reality.
