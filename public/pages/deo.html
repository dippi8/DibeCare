    About me

    Hi, I am Deo! Everyone here knows me for my green thumb, I am the wizard of the plants. If your guy is interested or simply curious about plants, flowers or gardening in general, I will be pleased to introduce him/her to this fantastic world.

    Also, I have a huge passion for books and stories in general. I have always loved reading, listening and also wrting them, since I was young. During the Group Reading activity our guys listen to stories read by me (or occasionally by other kids) and at the end of the session they can comment, ask questions and interact each other in a constructive way. We have a huge variety of books genres, and sometimes our guys can propose what to read together.
